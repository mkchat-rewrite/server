Rules:

  1. Slurs: (Users should refrain from sending anything that could be considered a slur while using mkchat. The use of these words is strongly prohibited, even if they are used in an inoffensive manner. Attempts to bypass mkchat word filters and made-up slurs used in an offensive manner are also included in this rule).
    1st infraction - 2 day ban
    2nd infraction - 1 week ban
    3rd infraction - 2 week ban
  2. RP in Public Rooms: (Users should not roleplay (RP) in any public rooms. If you wish to RP you can join a private room or room rp).
    1st infraction - warning
    2nd infraction - 2 day ban
    3rd infraction - 1 week ban
  3. NSFW: (No nsfw content should be sent on mkchat. This includes general nsfw content, messages/rp, images, and links).
    1st infraction - 2 day ban
    2nd infraction - 1 week ban
    3rd infraction - 2 week ban
  4. Spam: (No form of spamming will be tolerated on mkchat. This includes repeated message spam, sending overly large messages, text walls, copy-pastas, and spam joining).
    1st infraction - 2 day ban
    2nd infraction - 1 week ban
    3rd infraction - 2 week ban
  5. Impersonation: (Users should not copy the names of staff, discord users, or other chat users, especially when attempting to deceive other members).
    1st infraction - warning
    2nd infraction - 1 day ban
    3rd infraction - 1 week ban
  6. Illegal Activity: (This includes acts of or discussion about hacking, botting, DOS/DDOS attacks, and any other form of illegal activities).
    All infractions - permanent ban
  7. Threats: (Any form of threats toward other members are unacceptable).
    All infractions - permanent ban
  8. Ban Evasion: (Any attempt to evade bans will result in one week being added to your ban time. After many infractions you will be permanently banned).
